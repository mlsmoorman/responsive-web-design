About Us

We are the Buffalo Bison Dance Team in Buffalo, MN. Our team includes Varsity and JV groups. We are looking forward to an incredible 2023-2024 season!

Fall Season

BDT has a Fall Dance Team that runs from August - October and we perform at our Buffalo Football home games.

Fundraising

Our team does many fundraising events to help support the team every year. Funds go to support costumes, conference fees, coach fees and dance apparel.
